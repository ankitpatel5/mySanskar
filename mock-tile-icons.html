Set A — Outline
Multi-colour, each tile its own hue. Most common pattern in modern apps. Immediately clear what each section is about.

Different shapes, warmer icons. Heart for conversations feels more emotionally resonant. Vinyl disc for music is distinctive.

Most unique — diya/lamp for stories ties to the spiritual theme. Two-bubble conversation icon reads immediately as dialogue.
Set D — Monochrome
Single green accent throughout. Most restrained — premium feel, but individual icons may be less instantly recognisable.
