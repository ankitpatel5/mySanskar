Faces & Bodies
Let's talk about the parts of your face
Touch each part gently as you name it — eyes, nose, mouth, ears. Babies who hear body words early say them first.
Let's talk about your tiny hands
Count each finger slowly and wiggle them. Repetition helps pattern recognition form in their brain.
Let's talk about your big beautiful eyes
Hold your face 8–12 inches away — that's a newborn's focus range. Your eyes are their favourite thing to look at.
