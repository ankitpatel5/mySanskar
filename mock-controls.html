Recommendation

Option A (Size Hierarchy) is the most principled choice вЂ” it directly mirrors Apple Podcasts and Castro, both of which made this design decision after years of iteration. The three-tier sizing (40 В· 52 В· 66) immediately communicates what matters most. The circular skip icon with "15" inside is the universal standard that users already know from every major podcast app, so no learning curve.

The main change from current: replace the pill-shaped text buttons with the circular icon format. This removes a row of UI and the skip actions become equal citizens in the controls row.
