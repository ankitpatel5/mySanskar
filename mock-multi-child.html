Open Decisions to Confirm
1. Switcher placement
Topbar pill (shown here) vs. profile icon in topbar that opens a combined account + child switcher. Topbar pill is faster to reach but adds visual weight.
2. Story of the Day scope
Generate SOTD for the active child only (shown here) — simple. Or generate for all children and show multiple tiles. Multiple tiles is more impressive but requires layout work.
3. Saved stories — shared or per-child?
Currently one pool. With multi-child, mixing stories gets confusing fast. Recommend tagging each story with a child ID and adding a filter row in the saved stories tab.
4. Switching child → does SOTD regenerate?
If Arjun's story is already cached for today and you switch to Priya, should the SOTD immediately start generating Priya's story, or wait for the next day?
5. Max children
Recommend capping at 5 to keep the switcher and chip list manageable.
